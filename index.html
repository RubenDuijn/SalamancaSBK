FIND YOUR STYLE

Rhythms for Every Level

Choose the rhythm that suits you best and build confidence step by step in a lively, welcoming environment.

Energetic & social

Salsa

Energetic partnerwork, musicality, and a social atmosphere that gets people onto the dance floor fast.

Smooth & expressive

Bachata

Smooth, expressive, and playful, with room for beginners and experienced dancers alike.

Elegant & flowing

Kizomba

Flow, elegance, and connection for dancers looking to expand their range and musicality.

Precise & dramatic

Tango

Grounded connection, elegant walking, and rich musical detail for dancers who enjoy focus, control, and depth.

Fluid & creative

Zouk

Smooth turns, elastic flow, and expressive movement that give dancers space to explore softness, dynamics, and style.

Warm & joyful

Forró

A close, upbeat Brazilian social dance with playful footwork, easy groove, and the unmistakable feeling of a great party.

ABOUT SALAMANCA SBK

What Salamanca SBK Is Building

Salamanca SBK brings together Latin partner dance in Salamanca through classes, events, and a social scene that feels open, energetic, and easy to join. The focus is simple: strong fundamentals, good music, and a community people genuinely enjoy returning to.

Come for the rhythm, stay for the people, and keep building your confidence on the dance floor.
